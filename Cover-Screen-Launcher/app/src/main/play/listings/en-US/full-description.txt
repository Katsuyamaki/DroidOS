<p>DroidOS Launcher is a FOSS launcher module focused on windowed multitasking workflows on Android (external displays, AR displays, and cover screens).</p>

<p>Features include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Queue-based launch workflows</li>
<li>Multi-window tiling layouts (where supported by the device/configuration)</li>
<li>Companion integration with DroidOS Trackpad for input workflows</li>
</ul>

<p>Note: on many devices, full functionality requires Shizuku.</p>

<p>Documentation and source: https://github.com/Katsuyamaki/DroidOS</p>
